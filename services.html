Our Services

We handle HVAC, refrigeration, and cooking equipment — everything mechanical that keeps a commercial kitchen running.

Commercial HVAC

Restaurant kitchens generate serious heat. A standard office HVAC system can't handle it — and an improperly ventilated kitchen is a safety hazard, a health code issue, and a miserable place to work. We install, repair, and maintain commercial HVAC systems built for the real conditions of a working kitchen.

  • Rooftop unit (RTU) installation, repair, and maintenance
  • Kitchen exhaust hood and ventilation systems
  • Make-up air unit (MAU) installation and balancing
  • Commercial heating systems and furnaces
  • Air conditioning repair and seasonal maintenance
  • Ductwork inspection and modification
  • Thermostat and control system diagnostics
  • Preventive maintenance agreements

Commercial Refrigeration

  • Reach-in refrigerator and freezer service
  • Ice machine repair, cleaning, and maintenance
  • Refrigerated prep tables and pizza prep units
  • Display coolers and glass-door merchandisers

  • Temperature monitoring and alarm systems
  • Door gasket replacement and insulation repair

Preventive Maintenance

The cheapest repair is the one you never need. Regular maintenance extends equipment life, improves efficiency, and keeps you on the right side of health inspections.

Scheduled Check-Ups

Quarterly or seasonal visits to inspect, clean, and tune your equipment before something goes wrong. We work around your hours, not ours.

Filter & Coil Cleaning

Clogged filters and dirty coils are the #1 reason HVAC and refrigeration units fail early. Regular cleaning keeps them running efficiently and extends their lifespan.

Documentation

Need records for the health department or your insurance? We document every visit so you have proof your equipment is maintained to standard.
